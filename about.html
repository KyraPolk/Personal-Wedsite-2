Hobbies and Pass-times

Hiking

I enjoy long walks through beautiful landscapes and scenery simply breathing in fresh air and enjoying nature. It's not only a great exercise, but it's good for connecting to world in a new way.

Spending time with the Goodest of Boys

I have two very spoiled rotten dogs: "Blazer"(left) and "Chewie"(right). They are a purebred rottweiler and a weenie mix. They are a handful but super sweet and loving. I like to take them for walks and make them fetch their toys.

Gardening

I enjoy gardening a lot when its not 106 degrees in Louisiana. I have two lemon trees that I pick my own lemons from and I grow peppers and other veggies in the springtime as well.
